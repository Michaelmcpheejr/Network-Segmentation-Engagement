# Methodology

## Approach

The assessment followed a structured methodology designed to simulate a real-world GRC engagement.

### 1. Evidence Collection
- VLAN configurations
- Trunk configurations
- Routing tables
- Firewall zones and policies

### 2. Engineer Interview
A structured interview was conducted to:
- Validate segmentation intent
- Identify control assumptions
- Understand operational priorities

### 3. Configuration Analysis
- Compared intended design vs actual configuration
- Identified gaps in segmentation enforcement
- Evaluated exposure across network layers

### 4. Risk Identification
Each finding was evaluated using:
- Likelihood (1–5)
- Impact (1–5)
- Risk Score (Likelihood × Impact)

### 5. Framework Mapping
Findings were mapped selectively to:
- NIST 800-53
- NIST CSF
- CIS Controls
- MITRE ATT&CK

## Key Principle
Segmentation must be enforced through technical controls, not just defined logically.
